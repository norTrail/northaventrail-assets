Northaven Trail Map – Dallas Walking, Running & Bike Trail

This interactive Northaven Trail map shows the full route of the Northaven Trail in North Dallas, Texas, including access points, bridges, nearby streets, and points of interest. The trail is a paved, shared-use path designed for walking, running, and biking.

Northaven Trail Map View

List of Trail Points of Interest

Here is a full list of our trail’s points of interest.

Northaven Trail Access Points

The Northaven Trail can be accessed at multiple locations across North Dallas. Select an access point below to learn more about nearby neighborhoods, amenities, and trail connections.

  • Webb Chapel Road Access Point

  • Royal Park Access Point

  • Semones YMCA Access Point

  • Inwood Road Access Point

  • Preston Road Access Point

  • Freda Stern Access Point

  • US 75 / Central Expressway Access Point

Is the Northaven Trail paved?

Yes. The Northaven Trail is fully paved and suitable for walking, running, biking, strollers, and wheelchairs.

What can you find on the Northaven Trail map?

  • Trail route and length

  • Bridges and creek crossings

  • Entry points and neighborhood access

  • Drinking fountains and rest areas

  • Gardens and murals
